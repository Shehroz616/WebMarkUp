  • Alexander Nevsky Cathedral

    The Alexander Nevsky Cathedral is a majestic Neo-Byzantine and Neo-Russian masterpiece, known for its golden domes and intricate mosaics. Built to honor Russo-Bulgarian solidarity after the Russo-Turkish War, it's a symbol of gratitude. Inside, its opulent interior dazzles with marble, frescoes, and a remarkable iconostasis. Don't miss the adjacent bell tower, offering panoramic city views. This active place of worship is both an architectural marvel and a cultural symbol, showcasing Sofia's rich heritage.
