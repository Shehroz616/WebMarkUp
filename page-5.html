  • Borisova Gradina Park

    Borisova Gradina Park, nestled in the heart of Sofia, is a cherished urban retreat. This lush oasis boasts serene landscapes, a picturesque lake, and abundant greenery. It's a hub for recreational activities, offering sports facilities, family-friendly attractions, and cultural gems. With seasonal beauty, cultural events, and tranquil escapes, the park provides a harmonious blend of nature and city life. Come discover the serenity of Borisova Gradina, where Sofia's spirit finds refuge amidst the greenery.
