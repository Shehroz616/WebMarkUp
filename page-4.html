  • Sofia History Museum

    The Sofia History Museum offers visitors a captivating journey through the city's rich and diverse history. Housed in a beautifully restored historic building, this museum showcases artifacts, exhibitions, and interactive displays that highlight Sofia's evolution from ancient times to the present day. It provides insights into the city's cultural, social, and architectural heritage, making it a must-visit destination for history enthusiasts and anyone eager to uncover the secrets of Sofia's past.
