  • Ivan Vazov National Theater

    The Ivan Vazov National Theater, an architectural marvel, is Sofia's cultural heart. Named after poet Ivan Vazov, it celebrates Bulgaria's literary heritage. With a diverse repertoire, it hosts classical and contemporary performances. Beyond theater, it fosters intellectual and artistic engagement through exhibitions and discussions. A symbol of national pride, it offers guided tours to explore its opulent interiors and rich history. In essence, it's where culture and the arts flourish in Sofia.
